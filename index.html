Welcome to OpenDonation

We're an open-source platform built on the belief that giving should be simple, transparent, and impactful. We connect passionate donors, volunteers, and collaborators with charitable causes, both globally and in their local communities.

Our mission is to empower everyone to make a difference. We're not just a platform; we're a community dedicated to building a better world through collective action and open technology.

Our Core Values

  • ✔ Transparency: See exactly where your contribution goes.
  • ✔ Community: Join a global network of changemakers.
  • ✔ Impact: Support verified causes that deliver real results.

Our Mission

To make charitable giving and volunteering accessible to everyone, everywhere, by providing a free, transparent, and open-source platform for good.

Empower Communities

Provide tools for both global causes and local mutual-aid groups to raise funds and find support.

Ensure Transparency

Leverage open-source technology to create a clear path from donation to impact, building unwavering trust.

Support All Causes

From environmental protection and education to disaster relief and healthcare, we support all verified causes.
